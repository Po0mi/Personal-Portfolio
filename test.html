Design System Notes

Typography:
- Headlines: Bold condensed sans-serif (similar to your current design)
- Body: Clean sans-serif with proper line-height (1.6-1.8)
- Metadata: Small caps, 10-12px, letter-spacing increased
- Accent: Italic serif for emphasis words

Color Palette:
- Primary: #0a0a0a (deep black)
- Accent: #ff9500 (orange)
- Background: #f5f5f0 (warm off-white)
- Text: #333333 (dark gray)
- Metadata: #999999 (light gray)

Layout Elements:
- Grid coordinates and crop marks in corners
- Large outlined background text (like "MELANCHOLIC SOFTNESS")
- Plus (+) symbols as positioning markers
- Section numbers (01, 02, 03)
- Vertical text along edges
- Grain texture overlay on images
- Subtle paper texture on backgrounds

Interactive Elements:
- Cursor coordinates (top-right corner)
- Smooth scroll indicators
- Hover states reveal additional metadata
- Magnetic button effects
- Parallax on background elements
